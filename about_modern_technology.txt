Modern technology refers to advanced tools, systems, and methods, often rooted in post-WWII ICT, like AI, IoT, cloud computing, and biotech, that leverage scientific knowledge to automate tasks, connect people globally, and boost efficiency in all sectors, fundamentally transforming daily life but also posing new ethical & societal challenges. Key examples include artificial intelligence, robotics, 3D printing, quantum computing, and sustainable technologies, building upon or replacing older tech for better functionality.
